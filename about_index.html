About the Project

I composed a list of revolutions, revolts, uprsings, etc, under/against Tsarist Russia. The list stretches from the 17th century to the early 20th, from the Time of Troubles caused by the reign of Tsar Ivan IV (Often known as Ivan the Terrible), the first Russian tsar, to Tsar Nicholas II, the last. Included is also some basic details of the specific event, including dates, locations, reasons, results, and more. I may have missed a couple within this period, but I tried to be both thorough and accurate while doing my research, and I apologize in advance for any mistakes.
